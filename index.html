How it works

From lens to language
in four steps.

01 / Capture
📷
Camera
CameraX captures the frame. Flash assist, guide overlay, or import from gallery. Full control.
02 / Detect
🤖
YOLOv8-nano
On-device TFLite model detects all 26 braille letters at 320×320 with 96.76% mAP@50.
03 / Refine
🔤
SymSpell
Post-processing spell correction cleans detection artifacts. Smart dictionary lookup.
04 / Speak
🔊
TTS Engine
Android TTS reads the text aloud. Adjustable rate and pitch. Auto-speak option available.

Roadmap

Expand SymSpell Dictionary
Scale from ~150 words to 10,000+ for dramatically improved post-processing accuracy on real-world braille text.
Dark Mode Support
Full system dark mode integration with Compose theming. Retain the neo-brutalist character in both themes.
Batch Scanning Mode
Multi-page document scanning with automatic stitching. Scan an entire braille book, one page at a time.
PDF Export
Export your recognized text history as a formatted PDF. Share, print, or archive your scan sessions.
Multi-Language Braille
Grade 1 and Grade 2 braille support. International braille alphabet support beyond standard English a–z.
TalkBack Deep Integration
Full Android accessibility service integration. Make BrailleVision a first-class citizen for screen reader users.
